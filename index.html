OUR WORK

Perfect Blue

We worked on the psychological horror atmosphere, focusing obsessively on Mima's micro-expressions. We used subtle shifts in color and shadow to visually blur the boundary between reality and the protagonist's hallucinations.

Evangelion

Our role was giving weight and biology to the EVAs, animating them with animalistic, hunched movements rather than rigid mechanical ones. We also developed the experimental visual sequences in the later stages to reflect the characters' psychological instability.

Akira

We focused on effects animation and lighting, crafting the iconic light trails from the bikes and the detailed destruction of Neo-Tokyo. Our effort was poured into maintaining extreme fluidity in every frame to achieve that visceral, gritty realism.

Cowboy Bebop

We defined the style through action choreography and rhythm. We handled the fluid Jeet Kune Do fight animations and the integration of spaceships with the backgrounds, ensuring every movement was perfectly synchronized with the jazz soundtrack.
